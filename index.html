Why EAEA Awards?

The East Africa Entertainment Awards (EAEA) were created to celebrate and uplift talented individuals in the entertainment industry across East Africa. From music and film to fashion and digital media, EAEA serves as a bridge connecting creatives to global opportunities.

Voting

Fans have the power! Voting is open to the public and allows fans to support their favorite artists and entertainers. Stay tuned for the official voting period and instructions.

Our Mission

To recognize, celebrate, and promote excellence in the entertainment sector across East Africa by providing a credible and inclusive awards platform.

History of EAEA

Since its inception, the EAEA Awards have honored hundreds of outstanding individuals who have made significant contributions to the creative industry. The event continues to grow, inspiring the next generation of talent.
